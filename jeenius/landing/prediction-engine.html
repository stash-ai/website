3. The Trap Engine: Engineering Distractors

Real JEE questions are defined by their wrong options. Our Template & Distractor Engine recreates the traps examiners love:

  • Misconception Traps: Bait answers built from common conceptual slips.
  • Near-Miss Math: Options created from sign errors, conversion slips, or “halfway” algebra.
  • Boundary Bait: Edge-case answers focusing on limits, degeneracies, or extreme values.
  • Partial-Solution Lures: Answers that match the step before completion.

4. The Prediction Loop

  1. Blueprint Generation: Syllabus hierarchy + weightage forecasts decide the topic mix.
  2. Drafting: GPT-5 and Gemini models craft the stem, solution, and tone.
  3. Scrubbing: o-Series and Fathom AI audit units, diagrams, logic, and difficulty.
  4. Validation: Similarity guards keep the set original, not recycled.
